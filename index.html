üéÆ 1. Overview

The game is played between 2 to 4 players.

A game lasts about 30 minutes.

The player who made the highest score with both dice begins, and then the player to his left continues and so on. The goal being to reach 50 points, the players take turns.

First move : The player rolls two dice and moves his piece to the square. When a player comes across a simple 'Quizz' box, they must scan the QR code of the rules and then scroll down the page to land on 'Launch game'. By clicking on it, he will land on a button and will have to click on it so that fate will assign him a random difficulty (between EASY, MEDIUM & HARD).

‚≠ê EASY card

EASY card: if the player answers well to the question, he gains 2 points. if the player answers wrong to the question, he does not lose points.

‚≠ê‚≠ê MEDIUM card

MEDIUM card: if the player answers well to the question, he gains 4 points. If the player answers wrong to the question, he loses 2 points.

‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê HARD card

HARD card: if the player answers well to the question, he gains 6 points. if the player answers wrong to the question, he loses 5 points.

When a player comes across a 'Quizz' box with 'Choose', they will have to choose the level of difficulty they want, then draw a card on the board and respond to it.

When a player falls on a BONUS square, they earn 3 points. When a player falls on a MALUS square, they lose 2 points.

The first player to reach 50 points wins the game.
